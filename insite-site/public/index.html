About the Practice

One practitioner. Two worlds of care. A single standard: yours.

Most wellness practices ask you to choose between modern medicine and integrative healing. Insite Integrative Wellness refuses that choice. Here, the science of anesthesia and clinical outcomes lives alongside massage therapy, energy work, culinary medicine, and botanical care — because your body doesn't separate them, and neither should your care.

Every client relationship at Insite is structured around OPTIMIZE², a dual-performance framework that pairs practitioner-guided strategy with client-owned execution. It's how a hopeful intention becomes a sustained result.
