Your Privacy

Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 10, 2026

Insite Integrative Wellness ("we," "us," or "our") operates www.insiteintegrativewellness.com. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you visit our website, contact our office, or receive services at our Houston, TX location.

Information we collect

  • Contact information you provide directly, such as your name, email address, and mobile phone number when you request an appointment or contact us.
  • Service details you share with us, such as the services you are interested in and the message you send through our contact form.
  • Communications you exchange with our office by phone, text message, or email.

How we use your information

  • To schedule, confirm, and manage your appointments.
  • To respond to your questions and requests.
  • To send you text messages related to your care and appointments, where you have opted in to receive them.
  • To meet our legal, professional, and regulatory obligations.

SMS / text messaging

If you provide your mobile phone number, you are agreeing to receive text messages from Insite Integrative Wellness. Message & data rates may apply. Message frequency may vary. Reply HELP for more information. You can reply STOP or UNSUBSCRIBE at any time to opt out of receiving further text messages.

Sharing of information

No mobile details or personally identifiable information will be shared with third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes.

We may share information only as necessary to operate our practice — for example, with service providers who help us deliver scheduling or communications on our behalf and who are bound to protect your information — or where required by law. Text messaging originator opt-in data and consent will not be shared with any third parties.

Health information

Where Dr. Thompson provides clinical care, including anesthesia services, protected health information is handled in accordance with applicable law, including HIPAA, and is governed by the applicable Notice of Privacy Practices provided at the point of care.

Data security and retention

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect your information, and we retain it only as long as needed for the purposes described above or as required by law.

Your choices

  • Opt out of text messages at any time by replying STOP or UNSUBSCRIBE.
  • Request access to, correction of, or deletion of your contact information by emailing info@insiteintegrativewellness.com.
